Pampered Pets Risk Assessment and Mitigation Report - Team Notes

Our work on the Pampered Pets risk assessment was a highly coordinated effort.

We relied on Microsoft Teams as our main communication platform, allowing for

real-time discussions and clarifications. OneNote acted as our collaborative workspace, where we tracked

progress and shared notes, ensuring that all team members were aligned on tasks and deadlines.

We held regular team meetings every Monday to review progress, tackle any challenges,

and refine our approach.

These meetings were essential in keeping us on track and ensuring that each team

member—Gesine, Mauricio, Farhad, Tobias, and myself—could contribute effectively. Our approach combined clear

communication, structured follow-ups, and regular check-ins, which enabled us to deliver a well-organized and

thoroughly analyzed risk assessment report.

One of the significant challenges we faced was maintaining the 1000-word limit while

providing detailed analysis for each aspect of the risk assessment. However, through constant review and collaboration,

we were able to edit the report to remain within the required limits, ensuring we retained the essential points

without sacrificing critical content. Careful editing and teamwork were crucial in preserving the quality of our

insights while meeting the constraints.
